### General Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is very friendly and open. The members and mentors at FCAJ are extremely friendly and always ready to support when I encounter difficulties. The workspace is clean, tidy, and comfortable. There is a cafeteria for everyone to rest and have lunch, which helps me enjoy a wonderful lunch break.

**2. Support from Mentors / Team Admins**  
The mentors supported me and my peers very enthusiastically, always reviewing everyone's architectural diagrams and providing very detailed feedback. Thanks to the guidance and comments from the admin team, it helped me complete my work faster and more easily.

**3. Alignment Between Work and Major**  
Although the assigned work did not closely match the knowledge I learned at school, thanks to that, I was able to explore and learn a lot of new knowledge and practical skills.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills such as using project management tools, teamwork skills, and professional communication methods in a corporate environment. The mentors also shared many practical experiences to help me better orient my career.

**5. Culture & Team Spirit**  
The company culture is very positive: everyone respects each other, working seriously yet cheerfully. When an upcoming event is about to be organized, the admin team and I cooperate to help each other prepare in the best way possible.

**6. Policies / Benefits for Interns**  
The company supports internship students for 3 months with a requirement of only attending 10 sessions and 3 events in total. We can freely register our schedule to come to the office. This helps me and everyone not to worry about time constraints during the internship; if busy or having sudden reasons like illness, we do not need to register to come to the office and can study at home without needing to be physically present. This brings mental comfort to us interns as we are not bound by strict time constraints. Every month, the admin team organizes a meeting to discuss and resolve any questions or concerns from students.
